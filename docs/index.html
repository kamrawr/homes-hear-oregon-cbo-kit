Oregon Home Energy Rebate Programs

A guide for Community-Based Organizations administering HOMES and HEAR programs

About These Programs

Oregon is implementing two federal home energy rebate programs created by the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA):

üè† HOMES

Home Efficiency Rebates

Performance-based rebates for whole-home energy efficiency retrofits that achieve 20% or 35% modeled energy savings.

  • Up to $8,000 for households earning 80-150% AMI
  • Up to $8,000 for low-income households (<80% AMI)
  • Can be retroactive to Aug 16, 2022
Learn More

‚ö° HEAR

Home Electrification and Appliance Rebates

Point-of-sale rebates for electric appliances, heat pumps, and necessary electrical upgrades.

  • Up to $14,000 for low-income households (<80% AMI)
  • Up to $8,000 for moderate-income (80-150% AMI)
  • Cannot be retroactive
Learn More
